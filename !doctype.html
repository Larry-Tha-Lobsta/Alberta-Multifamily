1. Market Snapshot

  • Sentiment: Investor interest in Alberta remains relatively constructive, with buyers still attracted to positive population growth and a comparatively affordable cost base.
  • Pricing: Stabilized, well-located product in Edmonton and Calgary is generally transacting in a mid–4% to low–5% cap rate range, with older stock and secondary locations pricing wider.
  • Debt: All-in CMHC-insured coupons on 5–10 year terms are roughly in the mid–3% to low–4% area in this test example, depending on leverage and covenant strength.

Quick take: This is a test run using illustrative numbers only. In a live edition, this section would highlight what changed vs. last month on pricing, sentiment, and financing.

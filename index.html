1. Market Snapshot

  • Sentiment: Investor interest in Alberta remains constructive, supported by relative affordability and continued demand for rental product.
  • Underwriting tone: More attention on DSCR durability, concessions, and “new tenant” rent assumptions as supply delivers across several nodes.
  • Big picture: The market is more balanced than 2022–2024, but well-located assets with clear capex visibility continue to clear financing hurdles.
Quick take: Expect a wider gap between “A locations / strong physicals” and everything else. Execution and realism matter more than straight-line pro-forma growth.

7. How We’re Structuring Deals Right Now

  • Stabilized core assets: Target leverage that clears DSCR under conservative vacancy and defensible “new tenant” rents.
  • Value-add / lease-up: Underwrite in-place as the base case; treat upside as optionality supported by comps and a realistic timeline.
  • Conventional term debt: Use when speed/flexibility matters or CMHC fit is weak, with careful attention to refinance paths.
